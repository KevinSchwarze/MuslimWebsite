Adhkar

  • Bismillah (بِسْمِ اللهِ) - In the name of Allah
  • Alhamdulillah (الحمد لله) - Praise be to Allah
  • Yarhamukallah (يرحمك الله) - May Allah have mercy on you
  • Tabarakallah (تبارك الله) - Blessed is Allah
  • Assalamu Alaykum (السلام عليكم) - Peace be upon you
  • Wa Tabaarka Ismuka (وتبارك اسمك) - And blessed is Your name
  • Allahu Akbar (الله أكبر) - Allah is the Greatest
  • Subhanallah (سبحان الله) - Glory be to Allah
  • Astaghfirullah (أستغفر الله) - I seek forgiveness from Allah
  • Insha'Allah (إن شاء الله) - If Allah wills
  • Jazakallah Khair (جزاك الله خير) - May Allah reward you with goodness
  • La ilaha illallah (لا إله إلا الله) - There is no god but Allah
  • Subhanahu wa ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى) - Glorified and Exalted is He
  • La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah (لا حول ولاقوة إلا بالله) - There is no power nor strength except with Allah
  • Hasbunallahu wa ni'mal wakeel (حسبنا الله ونعم الوكيل) - Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs
  • Alhamdulillah Rabbil 'Alamin (الحمد لله رب العالمين) - Praise be to Allah, the Lord of all the worlds
  • Rabbi zidni ilma (ربي زدني علما) - My Lord, increase me in knowledge
  • Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un (إنا لله وإنا إليه راجعون) - Indeed, we belong to Allah, and indeed to Him we will return
  • Barakallahu feek (بارك الله فيك) - May Allah bless you
  • Rahimakallah (رحمك الله) - May Allah have mercy on you
